BirdSense

About

BirdSense is an innovative app that uses deep learning to identify bird species from photos instantly. Leveraging Keras and TensorFlow, it offers precise bird identification at your fingertips. Discover the joy of bird watching: download BirdSense today!

Enjoy our extensive gallery with more than 90.000 bird images and easily upload your own bird photos to get started. Join our community of bird enthusiasts and share your sightings!
